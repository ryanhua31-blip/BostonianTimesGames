How To Play

Rules for Bostonian Times Word Links

  • The board is a 15 by 15 grid with two starter words already placed.
  • Each new word must run horizontally or vertically.
  • Every move must overlap the existing board in exactly one matching letter.
  • The rest of the word extends the route, and touching letters continue the chain.
  • Each added word costs 10 points plus 1 point per letter.
  • Use the fewest, shortest words you can to build a connection.
  • Tap any square to prefill the row and column for your next move.
